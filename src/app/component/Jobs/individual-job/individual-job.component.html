Job Description

Summary

Develop user-centric, visually pleasing interfaces for web and mobile applications, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable user experience. Embrace an agile approach to design projects, responding adeptly to changing requirements and priorities while upholding design excellence and meeting deadlines.

Responsibilities

  1. Develop user-centric, visually pleasing interfaces for web and mobile applications, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

  2. Embrace an agile approach to design projects, responding adeptly to changing requirements and priorities while upholding design excellence and meeting deadlines.

  3. Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including developers, product managers, and stakeholders, to transform concepts and user stories into interactive designs.

  4. Participate in user research and usability testing to gain insights into user behaviour, needs, and preferences, using this information to inform your design decisions.

Requirements

  1. A degree in Interaction Design, UI/UX Design, or a related field.

  2. Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, including Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.

  3. Strong understanding of user-centred design principles and methodologies.

Benefits

  1. A degree in Interaction Design, UI/UX Design, or a related field.

  2. Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, including Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.

  3. Strong understanding of user-centred design principles and methodologies.
